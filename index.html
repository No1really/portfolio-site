About Me

About Me

I was born to a Bengali Army Officer's family in Rajasthan on 26th of November, 2004. Was brought up in Kolkata, did my schooling from here. While i was figuring out my interests and preferences during the final few years of school, my passion about learning how brands advertise and how easily people are attracted towards them grew. Besides this, I always had a passion about Music Production, ever since I was in my 8th standard.

Taking my interests about creativity, marketing and startup culture in, I decided to delve deeper and get a degree. That is when I came across this course from Jain Deemed to be University, Bangalore where i was given the opportunity to understand how branding worked, how marketing was done creatively, how to run and emphasize the ads and branding elements by incorporating the ideas such as Color Psychology, Brand personification and identity creation alongside the traditional B-School knowledge about Supply Chain Management, Product Management, Operations Management, Research and Analytics, etc. all while being at home on a laptop. This in turn, gave me enough room to experiment with different AI Tools such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Zapier, Lovable AI, Gemini, Grok among others.

And now, i am looking forward to bringing all my knowledge to practice in the real world and help myself as well as B2C Startups grow in the field.
